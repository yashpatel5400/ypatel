About me

About me

Let me tell you a little bit about myself

First off, I'm not a web or front-end developer (so stop web developing at me). I'm instead interested and passionate about machine learning and artificial intelligence: you can find a showcase of some of my pertinent (and non-pertinent projects) below. Since I'm assuming few people actually want to read about my background, that's probably all I'm gonna say here. But wait, if you want more, feel free to explore my blog!

My skills

What I (think) I'm good at

Data Science

Much of my past experience was in mathematical modelling and analytics, from health regimes to cryptocurrency

Machine Learning

ML (specifically natural language processing) perfectly combines the aspects of math and CS that intrigue me towards awesome projects

Web Development

Even though my main interests are outside pure web development, many of the projects developed below are showcased on custom websites

Augmented Reality (AR) Development

Began developing in AR development for the Apple platforms with their recent release of the ARKit SDK

Cloud Services

Developed projects deployed using standard cloud services (EC2, EBS) and developer tools that use many of the more niche AWS services (SNS, SQS)

System Administration

Capable of setting up and maintaining Linux servers, specifically Ubuntu and RHEL, largely on the web end.
